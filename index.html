About euthanasia conference

The World Federation of Right to Die Societies is an international federation of associations that promote access to voluntary euthanasia. It holds regular international meetings on dying and death. The 2018 Conference will be held in the beautiful cosmopolitan city of Cape Town, one of the leading destinations in the world.

The World Federation of Right to Die Societies, founded in 1980, consists of 51 right to die organizations from 23 countries. The Federation provides an international link for organizations working to secure or protect the rights of individuals to self-determination at the end of their lives.
